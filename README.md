# 🚖 Uber Saudi Operations Intelligence Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

This Power BI project provides a comprehensive analysis of Uber's operational and driver performance across Saudi Arabia between 2020 and 2025.

The dashboard enables stakeholders to monitor revenue growth, trip activity, fleet utilization, payment behavior, and driver performance while identifying operational challenges and opportunities for business expansion.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

* Analyze revenue growth trends.
* Monitor trip distribution across cities.
* Evaluate fleet performance and utilization.
* Measure driver earnings and acceptance rates.
* Understand customer payment preferences.
* Support strategic decision-making through data-driven insights.

## 🔍 Key Insights

### Revenue Performance

✔ Revenue increased significantly between 2020 and 2025.

✔ Strong Year-over-Year Growth of 67%.

✔ Fleet mileage expanded alongside business growth.

### Geographic Insights

✔ Tabuk generated the highest revenue.

✔ Revenue concentration observed in a limited number of cities.

✔ Growth opportunities identified in lower-performing regions.

### Driver Performance

✔ UberX generated the highest earnings.

✔ Driver acceptance rates varied across cities.

✔ Performance gaps identified among drivers.

### Payment Analysis

✔ Cash remains the most used payment method.

✔ Digital payments contribute over two-thirds of total transactions.

---

## ⚠️ Data Challenges Identified

* Revenue outliers in certain cities.
* Geographic demand imbalance.
* Driver performance inconsistencies.
* High dependency on cash payments.
* Acceptance rate variations across cities.

---

## 💡 Recommendations

* Expand digital payment adoption.
* Improve fleet allocation strategies.
* Increase driver incentives in low-performing cities.
* Strengthen fraud detection and revenue validation processes.
* Develop city-specific growth strategies.
